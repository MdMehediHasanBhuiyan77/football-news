Argentina vs Brazil: Copa America final

Argentina win the Copa America title for the first time since 1993
as they beat Brazil 1-0 in the final at Maracana Stadium in Rio de
Janeiro. Angel Di Maria scored the only goal of the match.
Argentina’s win was a particular triumph for Barcelona striker Lionel Messi,
who picked up his first ever title in a blue and white shirt after more than
a decade of club and individual honours.
Messi, who missed a golden chance to score with two minutes remaining,
finished the tournament’s joint top goalscorer with four goals and was elected
joint best player along with Neymar
